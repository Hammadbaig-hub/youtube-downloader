Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's exactly what we collect and why.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive and store:

  • Name — displayed in your account
  • Email address — used to identify your account
  • Profile picture — displayed as your avatar
  • Google account ID — used to link your account securely

We also store download history records (video title, platform, quality, date) linked to your account.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not store the actual downloaded video files on our servers
  • We do not collect payment information (no payments are currently processed)
  • We do not track your browsing activity outside of VidFlow
  • We do not sell your data to third parties

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session
  • To display your download history and usage statistics
  • To enforce download limits per account
  • To send transactional emails (welcome email on signup)

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secure database. Download history is retained for as long as your account exists. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

VidFlow uses the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth — for sign-in authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy

6. Cookies

VidFlow uses session cookies to keep you logged in. These cookies are essential for the Service to function and are deleted when you log out or your session expires.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to processing of your data

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. Download records older than 1 year may be automatically purged. You may delete your account at any time from your Account page.

9. Children's Privacy

VidFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries or data deletion requests, please contact us through the app.
